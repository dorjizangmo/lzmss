Lungtenzampa Middle Secondary School, formerly Thimphu Nursery School, is an important institution in Thimphu, Bhutan, known for its role in education and its commitment to Gross National Happiness (GNH) values. It began as a nursery school in 1972 and has evolved to its current status as a middle secondary school, offering classes up to class X. The school's vision is to create an educated and enlightened society that upholds GNH values.

Here's why Lungtenzampa Middle Secondary School is significant: Evolution and Growth: The school's journey from a nursery to a middle secondary school reflects its commitment to providing quality education and adapting to the growing needs of the community. GNH Values: Lungtenzampa MSS actively promotes and integrates GNH values into its educational approach, aiming to produce students who are not only academically successful but also contribute to a harmonious and sustainable society. Academic Excellence: The school has a strong track record of academic achievement, including a 100% pass rate in the BCSE exams for its first batch of students, with a significant number qualifying for higher secondary education. Holistic Development: Lungtenzampa MSS focuses on the holistic development of its students, encouraging participation in extracurricular activities and fostering dynamic global citizens. Community Engagement: The school actively participates in community events and initiatives, such as celebrating World Environment Day and organizing a Scouts Boot Camp, demonstrating its commitment to social responsibility. Positive School Culture: Lungtenzampa MSS fosters a positive learning environment, as evidenced by the testimonials and messages from students and staff who express pride and inspiration in their school.
